Welcome to FreeEdu!

We are a free peer tutoring service that aims to reduce educational inequality in the San Diego area. We provide one-on-one tutoring between high school tutors and students up to Grade 12. We offer online tutoring in many subjects, including Math, Chemistry, English, US and World History, Computer Science, and more. For more information on tutoring for specific subjects or courses, please visit our programs page or our contact us page.

How to sign up for tutoring:

  1. Click on the Register Now! tab
  2. Fill out the Google Form
  3. Receive an email response from us
  4. We will assign you a tutor who is suited to help you with the subject you need help in and whose schedule is compatible with yours
  5. You can then directly communicate with your tutor via email or text and arrange sessions and preferred platform (Google Meets, Zoom, Discord, etc.)
  6. If you have any questions or concerns, please let us know by contacting us through our email or phone number, which are listed on the Contact Us page.
