Welcome to FreeEdu!

We are a free peer tutoring service that aims to reduce educational inequality in the San Diego area. We provide one-on-one tutoring between high school tutors and students up to grade 11. We offer online tutoring in many subjects, including math, chemistry, English, US and World history, computer science, and more. For more information on tutoring for specific subjects or courses, please visit our programs page or our contact us page.
